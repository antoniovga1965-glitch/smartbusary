    The Process

    How it works

    • Step 1 — Apply

      Students submit their application online with personal details, school information, and supporting documents.

    • Step 2 — Sieves Run

      26 AI-powered fraud detection sieves instantly scan every application for inconsistencies, duplicates, and document tampering.

    • Step 3 — Verified or Flagged

      Clean applications are marked verified and forwarded. Suspicious ones are flagged with a detailed reason for human review.

    • Step 4 — County Reviews

      County bursary officers receive a clean dashboard showing verified applicants, flagged cases, and full audit trails.

    • Step 5 — Disbursement

      Approved students receive their bursary directly — with every transaction logged, traceable, and corruption-proof.

    Got Questions?

    FAQ Questions

    Is my data safe

    All applicant data is encrypted end-to-end and stored on secure servers. No personal information is shared with third parties. Only authorized county officials can access application records.

    Who can see my applications

    Only you and the assigned county bursary officer can view your application. All access is logged with timestamps creating a full audit trail.

    Has Smart Bursary ever been breached?

    Smart Bursary is built with security-first architecture including rate limiting, device fingerprinting, and encrypted databases. No breaches have occurred.

    Are my uploaded documents secure?

    All uploaded documents are stored with military-grade encryption. Documents are used strictly for verification purposes and cannot be accessed by unauthorized parties.
